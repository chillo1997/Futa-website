FutaPay

Send money from Europe to African mobile wallets with a clear, mobile‑first flow.

Instant payment links, QR codes, and transparent fees.
* Product details illustrative; adapt to your live corridors and fees.

FutaPay is the fastest way to send money to your family, friends, or business partners. The receiver creates a pay link and shares it with the sender via WhatsApp, SMS, or any chat app. The sender pays with iDEAL, SEPA, or cards, and funds are paid out to mobile wallets within minutes. Simple, transparent, and built for families and SMEs. Crossplatform made easy. From bank account to mobile wallet in minutes.
