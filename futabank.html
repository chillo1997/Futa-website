FutaBank

The future home for simple accounts and helpful business tools.

Roadmap ideas: local IBANs, savings pots, lightweight invoicing, and easy cash‑out options. Built with compliance in mind.
FutaBank (early stage) is a next-generation wallet built for how Africa moves—helping you manage balances, pay seamlessly, and move money across borders with confidence. We’re designing for flexibility and fairness: clear pricing, fast settlements, and a product that meets you where you are—at home, at work, or on the move. FutaBank is in active development; capabilities, coverage, and timelines may evolve as we validate with users and partners.
