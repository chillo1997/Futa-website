Nyota

Since antiquity, stars have helped humanity find its bearings—guiding travelers, setting our calendars, inspiring our myths, and symbolizing hope and eternity. The star once led the wise men to Bethlehem; today, Nyota (“star” in Swahili) points the way to Africa’s next chapter.

We build simple, trusted digital rails that show where we are, where we’re going, and how to get there—connecting people across borders with clarity and confidence. Through FutaPay, FutaBank, and NyotaMobile, and many other products. We make everyday finance and connectivity fast, transparent, and fairly priced for families, freelancers, and growing businesses.

Rooted in compliance and security, designed for usability, and built for scale, Nyota turns cross-border complexity into a tap.
